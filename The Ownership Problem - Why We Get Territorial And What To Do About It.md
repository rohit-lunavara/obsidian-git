> [!QUOTE] Original Capture
> Territorial behavior is one of the most common collaboration problems in Tech, and one of the least named. Because nobody thinks of themselves as territorial
> 
> How territorial behavior shows up:
> 1. Shutting down ideas before they get a fair hearing
> 2. Gatekeeping knowledge
> 3. Gatekeeping information flow and decision-making
> 
> What causes territorial behavior:
> 1. Lack of trust
> 2. Perceived lack of control
> 3. Being burned in the past
> 4. Job security
> 5. Overwhelm
> 
> What to do when others are being territorial
> 1. Lead with curiosity
> 2. Do your homework
> 3. Be transparent about what you want
> 4. Include them early and give them a real role
> 5. Name the problem, not the person
> 
> What to do when you’re the territorial one
> 1. Get curious before getting defensive
> 2. Engage with the idea, don't just shut it down
> 3. Be principled when you say no
> 4. Name what you actually need
